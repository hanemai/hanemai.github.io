Events

The events at our school are also quite fun. Here's some descriptions and pictures of them.






EBC Week 2022

EBC Week is a week before Spring Break where students go to different destinations in groups and learn outside of the classsroom.
These trips have a large variety, from D.C. to SF.





Homecoming 2021

This event is a high school dance that happens every year in the fall trimester. It's open to all of Upper School and generally has music, food, and games.





Winter Social 2022

The Winter Social is for Upper School students and took place at an ice rink where students were allowed
to skate freely with refreshments and equiptment provided.





Culture Night

Culture Night, organized by Allies for Equity, is an event where students can present and represent their culture.
This event includes booths and performances and also treats.
