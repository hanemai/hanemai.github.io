About

At NWABR we envision a future time where the public recognizes and values the contribution of ethically conducted biomedical research and where this research leads to significant improvement in the communities’ health.

NWABR works to achieve this Vision though the promotion of the public's trust in biomedical research, and its ethical conduct.
